 Vacation Rental Distribution News

August 21, 2014

Maxxton



Hospitality innovation and enterprise resource planning

A single database, one-time data entry and total information security.
Rather than running separate systems and databases for property management, central reservations and customer relationship management, Maxxton delivers an integrated, functional, stable and open enterprise resource planning solution. The company's flagship product Newyse provides complete management functionality, tools and applications in a single platform and database.

Streamlined -   Fast   -   Cost-effective


Why enterprise resource planning?
The challenge today is to determine the most efficient and cost-effective means of accessing, integrating and distributing corporate information across the organization. At the same time, guests, visitors and holidaymakers must be given the best customer experience possible. This will help increase repeat visits, positive recommendations and ultimately create brand ambassadors.
Maxxton was the first company in Europe to launch a fully integrated, cloud based enterprise resource planning system specifically for the hospitality industry. It facilitates faster and less costly implementation, lowers overall IT costs, enables you to easily exploit new technology and takes your business into the online world.
Maxxton's solution also reduces pressure on internal IT resources and optimizes operations by matching the requirements of today and scaling your solution into the future.
Driven by customer demand and industry trends, Newyse is powered by advances in product development, resulting in greater productivity and more responsiveness for your business.

How it all began
Netherlands-based Jean Pierre Mampaey began his career in hospitality at the tender age of 14 working as a barrier attendant at Roompot Beach Resort and Campsite. He then studied to be a systems analyst and went on to become Chief Operating Officer for two further resorts with campsites and a marina.
In 1998 he established what was to become Europe's premier consultancy for hospitality, leisure and recreational lodging, employing a team of 15 first-rate specialists. In his role Mampaey travelled extensively, all the time searching for the best and most favorable business management platform for complex holiday parks, resorts and campsites. Finding nothing that matched his concept of a single system resource, Mampaey deployed his own accomplished IT skills and developed Newyse. Today, Maxxton is the leading provider of hospitality ERP software in Europe.

At the forefront of industry developments and trends
Maxxton's founder is also chairman of Recron: an independent board in the Netherlands that analyzes hospitality trends. Mampaey utilizes this experience to the benefit of Maxxton's clients.
Newyse also enables information flow between internal business functions and manages connections with outside stakeholders (guests, tour operators, lodging owners and so on).
The product is proven to cut costs, streamline operations, increase efficiency, improve business performance and boost occupancy targets.
Businesses that use Newyse enjoy meaningful business grow, enriched customer experience and increased bookings while at the same time minimizing IT investment.
Newyse is being used by customers worldwide in the following sectors:

 - Independent hotels and holiday resorts.
 - Hotel and holiday resort chains.
 - Campground and RV parks.
 - Tour operators and online travel agents.
 - Vacation rentals and points systems providers.

A global IT partner
Maxxton's head office is in Middelburg in the Netherlands, where it all began. The company is now part of an international group, which includes support and development offices in India and the Netherlands and sales and client support offices in America, the United Kingdom and India.
