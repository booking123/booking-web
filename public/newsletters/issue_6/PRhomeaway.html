BookingPal Announces New Partnership with HomeAway Software
Vacation rental property managers using HomeAway Software can now have their inventory displayed on BookingPal’s global network of online channel partners.


Irvine, CA September 30, 2014
BookingPal™, the leader in vacation rental distribution, is pleased to announce a new partnership with HomeAway Software®, the professional management software arm of HomeAway, Inc. (NASDAQ: AWAY), and the leading provider of technology solutions for the vacation rental management industry.

Vacation rental property managers using HomeAway Software solutions ISILink ® and EscapiaNET℠ can now have their inventory displayed on BookingPal’s global network of online channel partners and distributed to offline travel agents that specialize in vacation rental properties. This expanded distribution enables property managers to increase their properties’ exposure, thus generating more bookings and revenue. BookingPal distributes vacation rental and short-term rental properties to online travel agencies (OTAs), vacation rental listing sites, travel meta-search sites and travel agents.

Brian Brown, VP of Business Development at BookingPal commented: “We are very excited to work with HomeAway Software and provide property managers using ISILink and EscapiaNET the opportunity to increase bookings for their properties worldwide from BookingPal channels. We are looking forward to a highly successful partnership.”

"HomeAway Software is committed to assisting vacation rental managers gain exposure to millions of travelers searching for vacation accommodations online," says Bill Furlong, vice president of HomeAway Software "By partnering with BookingPal, we are expanding the network of distribution sites and services available to our customers and ultimately creating even more value for customers."

About HomeAway Software
HomeAway Software is the leading provider of property management software for professional vacation rental managers. HomeAway Software solutions consist of powerful productivity tools that automate key vacation rental management tasks, streamline operations, and help vacation rental managers maximize their online potential through world-class websites, booking engines, and the industry’s leading distribution programs. HomeAway Software is part of HomeAway, Inc. (NASDAQ:AWAY) the world’s leading online marketplace of vacation rentals.

About BookingPal
BookingPal is a cloud based travel technology company, providing a global distribution system and a centralized booking platform for over 250,000 real time bookable vacation rental properties. Vacation rental and corporate housing property managers can increase their online presence and number of bookings by distributing their inventory via BookingPal’s global distribution channels. BookingPal was founded in 2013 and headquartered in Irvine, California. To learn more about BookingPal, please visit www.mybookingpal.com or email info@mybookingpal.com.
