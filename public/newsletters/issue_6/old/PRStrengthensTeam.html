BookingPal Announces New Partnership with Streamline Vacation Rental Software
BookingPal is pleased to announce a new partnership with Streamline Vacation Rental Software.


Irvine, CA March 11, 2015
BookingPal is pleased to announce a new partnership with Streamline Vacation Rental Software. Over the past year, BookingPal has become one of the world’s largest vacation rental distribution networks with over 250,000 bookable vacation homes worldwide.

This new partnership will allow property managers that utilize the Streamline Vacation Rental Software to have their inventory displayed on and distributed to our global network of online channels partners and offline travel agents that specialize in vacation rental properties. This expanded distribution enables the property managers to increase their property’s exposure, thus generating more bookings and revenue.

Brian Brown, VP of Business Development at BookingPal commented, “BookingPal is committed to providing property managers that use the Streamline Vacation Rental Software the opportunity to increase bookings for their properties worldwide from channels such as, Booking.com, FlipKey, Homes.com, Villas.com, and many more. We know this will be a highly successful partnership.”

“We’re excited that our customers will now be available to distribute their vacation rental properties through BookingPal’s online and offline distribution channels. This will increase bookings for our customers by making their properties much more visible to millions of travelers.” Carlos Corzo, CEO of Streamline.

As forward thinking companies, BookingPal and Streamline are committed to providing vacation rental companies and their customers with today’s most advanced technology to help maximize bookings for their vacation rental properties.



About Streamline
Streamline is a tool that allows property managers, from large resorts to homes, to simplify the rental process, optimizing their business. It brings about a greater efficiency in running any rental business, allowing for increased volume along with an increase in customer satisfaction. Streamline was designed with great consideration for the experience of the managers, owners and also the renters, in an attempt to make everyone’s rental experience more straightforward and stress-free.
The creation of this software was a result of the deficiencies of other software within the industry. While there are options in place for online rental management, none have the functionality and easy-to-use feel that Streamline provides. Our clients are able to manage all aspects of their business through a centralized hub.www.streamlinevrs.com

About BookingPal
BookingPal is a cloud based travel technology Company, providing a global distribution system and a centralized booking platform for short term rental properties. Short term rental property owners/managers can increase their online presence and number of bookings by distributing their inventory via BookingPal’s global distribution channels. Vacation rental properties, timeshare resorts, and corporate housing can use BookingPal’s guest generation capabilities to maximize profitability and increase efficiency of their online performance. Founded in 2013 and headquarters in Irvine, California, BookingPal currently handles over 250,000 online bookable units worldwide.
