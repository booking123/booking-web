Tips on choosing vacation home rentals for the holidays



DENVER - If you are looking for a vacation home rental for the holidays, be aware of red flags to consider. If you don't, a holiday vacation could turn into a holiday disaster.

Many families rent a vacation home for family gatherings over the holidays and it's important to know how to find and evaluate the best vacation rental homes so that a holiday isn't ruined by a bad choice.

Brian Corbett with Denver -based destination club Inspirato stopped by 9NEWS at 8:15 a.m. and shared some tips with those looking for vacation rentals.
