What Are 3rd Party Channels?



Different types:

Vacation Rental Sites, OTAs, Property Listing Sites, Meta-Search Sites/ Aggregators & Travel Agents

Models for Distribution





Vacation Rental Sites
Traditional Vacation Rental Distribution Channel

No. of Monthly Visits to the top 10 Sites: 43,122,000*

Vacation Rental Sites are the traditional type of distribution channel for rental homes. They display a selection of different vacation rental properties. Some sites have global coverage while others only show regional coverage.

Market Size: Thousands of Vacation Rental Sites worldwide

Financial Model: Pay per Listing or, Pay per Inquiry, moving into Pay per Booking

Two types of Vacation Rental Sites:



OTAs
New Players: Gradually moving into Vacation Rental Market

No. of Monthly Visits to the top 10 Sites: 257,776,000*

OTAs which stand for Online Travel Agent/ Agencies are internet-based travel websites, similar to an online version of the traditional travel agent. They specialize in offering planning sources and booking capabilities.

Market Size: Thousands of Vacation Rental Sites worldwide

Financial Model: Pay per Listing or, Pay per Inquiry, moving into Pay per Booking



Property Listing Sites
New Players: Gradually moving into Vacation Rental Market

No. of Monthly Visits to the top 10 Sites: 115,665,000*

Property Listing Sites are sites that display accommodations for sale as well as long term rental properties.

Market Size: Thousands of Property Listing Sites worldwide

Financial Model:Pay per Listing or, Pay per Inquiry, moving into Pay per Booking



Meta-Search Sites/ Aggregators
New Players: Gradually moving into Vacation Rental Market

No. of Monthly Visits to the top 10 Sites: 43,054,000*

Meta-Search engines enable users to search and compare a large inventory of accommodations across multiple sites. Travelers are then redirected to specific websites matching their criteria.

Market Size: Dozens of Meta-Search Sites/ Aggregators worldwide

Financial Model:PPC (Pay per Click basis)


Travel Agents
New Players: Travel agents are now looking to earn commission on Vacation Rentals

A Travel Agent is s private retailer or public service that provides travel related services to the public on behalf of suppliers such as vacation rentals.

Market Size: Over 700,000 travel agents worldwide

Financial Model:Pay per Booking
