August 21, 2014

Rental Roost



RentalRoost was founded on the mission of making house hunting fun and easy, after each of the co-­founders had trouble finding a perfect home and neighborhood after several out-­of-­state relocations. If we found a place that was dog-­friendly, it was not the most walkable neighborhood. When we found a fantastic neighborhood with many things within walking distance, it wasn’t pet-­friendly. And certain vibrant neighborhoods did not have the most family-friendly aspects such as good schools. It got us thinking. “Shouldn’t house-­hunting be much easier than this if everyone has to go through it at least once in their lifetime?” And voila, RentalRoost was born. 

With our personalized searches and easy-­to-navigate website, house hunting doesn’t need to be as tiring or boring as it is. You can explore new aspects of your favorite neighborhoods and properties, while discovering new places to live. And at the end of the day, you can save all your searches in one convenient portal with My RR. RentalRoost is like the fun quirky know-­it­-all personal assistant we all wish we had!

Our proprietary 360˚ search allows you to find your perfect home - whether it’s a townhouse within walking distance to public transit, an apartment with a high Walkscore® or a home within a district with fantastic public schools for your little ones. And the My RR page allows you to save all your searches, so that you can return to any search result that caught your eye, even if you have to step away from your house-­hunting process for a moment.
ABOUT US
BookingPal is a cloud based travel Technology Company, providing a global distribution system and a centralized booking platform for short term rental properties. Short term rental property owners/managers can increase their online presence and number of bookings by distributing their inventory via BookingPal’s global distribution channels. Vacation rental properties, timeshare resorts, and corporate housing can use BookingPal’s guest generation capabilities to maximize profitability and increase efficiency of their online performance. Founded in 2013 and headquarters in Irvine, California, BookingPal currently handles over 183,000 online bookable units worldwide.
